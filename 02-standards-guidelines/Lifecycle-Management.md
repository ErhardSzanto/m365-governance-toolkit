# Lifecycle Management (Teams / SharePoint / OneDrive)

## Policy
Every Team/Site must have:
- A named **Business Owner**
- At least one **Technical/Service Owner**
- A defined **purpose**
- A scheduled **review cadence**

## Lifecycle flow
1. **Request**
   - Requester submits purpose, owner(s), sensitivity level, external sharing need
2. **Approval**
   - Business owner approves purpose and ownership
   - Security/Compliance approval required for exceptions (e.g., wide external sharing)
3. **Provisioning**
   - Team/Site created using standard template (naming + baseline settings)
4. **Ownership**
   - Owners maintain membership, content structure, and compliance with standards
5. **Review cadence**
   - Every **6 months**: confirm owners, membership, external guests, and relevance
6. **Archiving / deletion**
   - If inactive for **180 days**, archive and restrict changes
   - After archive period (e.g., 90 days), delete if no business justification

## Ownership responsibilities
Owners must:
- Remove leavers promptly and manage guest membership
- Ensure correct data classification/labels are used
- Keep content organised and ensure appropriate permissions
- Participate in periodic reviews and respond to governance queries
